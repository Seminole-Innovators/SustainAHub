Invasive Plant Guide

Air Potato

Dioscorea Bulbifera

The Air Potato is a species of yam that is native to Africa, Asia, and some parts of Oceania. It grows very fast and smothers native vegetation. It is distinctive for its large, heart-shaped leaves and its bulbs that grow suspended from its vines.

Find out more

Australian Pine

Casuarina equisetifolia

The Australian Pine is a tree that was introduced to Florida for ornamental and windbreaking purposes. Its roots erode beach soil and interferes with the nest building activities of endanged American Crocodiles and sea turtles.

Find out more

Brazilian Pepper

Schinus terebinthifolius Raddi

The Brazilian Pepper is a species of tree that competes with native plants, overcrowding vegetation. Scientific research suggests that it may produce chemicals that kill native species.

Find out more

Chinese Tallow

Triadica sebifera

Chinese tallow is an ornamental vine that crowds out native species. Its decaying leaf litter poisons water, causing disruptions in the lifecycles of aquatic animals.

Find out more

Cogon Grass

Imperata Cylindrica

Cogon Grass is a species of rhizomatous grass that is native to parts of Africa, Asia, and Southern Europe. It forms dense strands that crowd out native plant species, thus threatening the ecosystem.

Find out more

Kudzu

Pueraria montana

Kudzu is a rapid-growing vine that crowds out native species and is one of the most destructive species to native vegetation.

Find out more

Melaleuca

Melaleuca quinquenervia

Melaleuca is an invasive tree that degrades wetlands in several ways, including altering fire regimes, water table depth, vertical structure of plant communities, recruitment of native species, and light availability

Find out more

Old World Climbing Fern

Lygodium microphyllum

The Old World climbing fern is a species of viney fern that smothers native plant species and increases fire risk by allowing fires to climb up trees

Find out more

Water Hyacinth

Eichhornia crassipes

The Water Hyacinth is a species of flower that blocks out sunlight from organisms living below the surface of the water and overcrowds aquatic habitats.
