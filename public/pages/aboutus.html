About Us

Our Patch was founded with a purpose that comes from the heart: protecting the environment through connecting humans to it. The project is focused specifically on the Tallahassee Metropolitain area. We everyone to engage with community organizations to learn how to contribute and connect to sustainability and environmental protection efforts. We see the world like a big, beautiful garden, and for us, Tallahassee is our patch. Let's all care for it together.
